01. The Core Engine

Yubee functions as a centralized B2B marketplace and inventory management layer. It bridges the gap between veterinary procurement and operational delivery. The architecture is built on a modular data-driven framework that ensures real-time tracking of medical assets.

The Yubee stack is optimized for high-volume veterinary environments. Our focus is on low-latency data retrieval and rigorous transactional accuracy, ensuring that clinic owners maintain full visibility over their digital procurement cycle.
